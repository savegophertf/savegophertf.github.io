--- title: "#SaveGopherTF Michael Weissenborn" layout: alumni_testimonial fullname: "Michael Weissenborn, '09 Student Manager" quote: "Embrace Dreams, Preserve Opportunities" image: "/images/testimonials/mweissenborn.jpg" ---

I was a student-manager for six years. I almost dropped out of college. The track team kept me going. I'm now leading an awesome life, working happily as a software engineer for one of the largest data analytics firms in the world.

I'm not where I am today without this team. Gutting the program would destroy the dreams of so many student-athletes, and it won't stop here - universities across the country are waiting to see what happens at Minnesota. We must do everything possible to preserve opportunities for these kids.
