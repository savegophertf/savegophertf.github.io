--- title: "#SaveGopherTF Stacy Klos Mitchell" layout: athlete_testimonial fullname: "Stacy Klos Mitchell, '93 Multi" quote: "Loss of talent and trust" image: "/images/testimonials/placeholder.jpg" ---

Track gave me a chance at a great college experience and transformed me to a confident, competent and prepared woman. The teammates I had enhanced my life immensely and still do! I knew as soon as I stepped on campus I wanted to be a Golden Gopher.

The tragedy is the fact that opportunity is lost for many athletes in sports that already take a backseat in popularity but lead in academics and integrity. The U will be missing out on some of the best student athletes and will not be trusted again to make a valid decision.
