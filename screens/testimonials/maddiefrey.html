--- title: "#SaveBrownTrack Maddie Frey" layout: athlete_testimonial fullname: "Maddie Frey '22 Sprints" quote: 'The "Excellence Initiative" will destroy one of the most diverse and tight-knit communities at Brown.' image: "/images/testimonials/MFrey.png" ---

Being a member of the Brown track and field team has provided me with a diverse, inclusive, and accepting community that is integral to my experience at Brown. Not only would that tight-knit community be torn apart by eliminating the men's program, I believe Brown as a whole would be impacted. The sport of track is an avenue through which low-income and POC students are able to receive an unparalleled education, but without half of our program, Brown's "commitment" to diversity will suffer.
