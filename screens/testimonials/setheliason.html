--- title: "#SaveGopherTF Seth Eliason" layout: athlete_testimonial fullname: "Seth Eliason, '22 Distance/XC" quote: "The history and passion of this team is a fundamental part of Minnesotan culture" image: "/images/testimonials/seliason.jpg" ---

Track and cross country have been one of the most, if not the most impactful parts of my life. I have made memories and friendships that I will cherish forever and I don’t think any of this would have been possible without the track program. I’m devastated young Minnesotans won’t have the same opportunity I have. The team has brought so much opportunity to a wide variety of people here in Minnesota and across the country, taking it away from all of us is a violation of the history of this program.
