--- title: "#SaveGopherTF Kevin Moorhead" layout: alumni_testimonial fullname: "Kevin Moorhead '78 Distance/XC" quote: "Let's keep the U's integrity to the world intact." image: "/images/testimonials/kmoorhead.jpg" ---

I went to the U to run for Coach Griak. I had yet to decide on a career path, but I knew that it had to include continuing my distance running. Coach Griak was a legend in my community, St. Louis Park as were the athletes he coached at Park High. Thanks to Coach, I am in my 44th year as a high school coach in Minnesota. Athletics, track and field played an integral part in the development of my career. Integrity was a key component in what Coach Griak preached. Let's hope the UM keeps its integrity and reinstates Men's Track & Field !
