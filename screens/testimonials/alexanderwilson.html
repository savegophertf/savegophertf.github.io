--- title: "#SaveBrownTrack Alexander Wilson" layout: athlete_testimonial fullname: "Alexander Wilson '22 Sprints" quote: "We ask the administration to listen to what we have to say, and truly see the impact their decision is having on an entire community." image: "/images/testimonials/AWIlson.png" ---

Track has always been a part of who I am and has helped me to become the man I am today. Although it is not the only reason I chose to attend Brown, I truly believe it has been essential in my development to become a better person. To see Track get cut is hard because I know the benefits it has for other African-American males looking to use it as a means to achieve a higher education.
