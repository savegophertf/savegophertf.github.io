--- title: "#SaveGopherTF Obsa Ali" layout: spotlight_testimonial fullname: "Obsa Ali, '19 Distance/XC" description: "This is a description" quote: "Representation At Every Level Matters" image: "/images/testimonials/oali.jpg" ---

To me, the University of Minnesota Track and Field team means the world. Growing up, I never imagined continuing my education post high school bc that was not something that was ever prioritized in the environment that I had grown up in. Going to college or perusing education post high school was something foreign until I was presented with the opportunity to run for one of the best schools in the entire country. University of Minnesota Track and Field program gave me the tools I needed. It put me on even playing field with the peers around me. Taking away this program means taking away opportunities from kids like me who went to a high school that was underfunded. By taking this program away, you are keeping those underrepresented kids marginalized.
