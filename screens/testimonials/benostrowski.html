I severely broke my leg after my sophomore year, and believed my track career was done. With the support and love of my teammates and coaches, I came back to run at HEPS my senior spring. I wouldn't have attended Brown if it weren't for track, I wouldn't have worked back to race if it weren't for my track family, and I wouldn't know that beautiful family if it weren't for the program. Bring it back.
